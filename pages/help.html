Rules

Firts : You can allow the first player to decide whether to go with "X"s or to go with "O"s

Second : After the first player goes, then the second player should put down his symbol, which will be different from the symbol of the first player.

Third : These symbols will be placed on the table, in an attempt to have three of them in a row.

Fourth : Keep alternating moves until one of the players has drawn a row of three symbols or until no one can win.
